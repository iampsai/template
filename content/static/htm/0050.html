Device Management

LESSON 6 of 9

We'll will discuss meaningful ways to manage your devices effectively to keep them secure and protect your data. Proper device management is crucial for preventing unauthorized access and ensuring your information remains intact. Here are two key strategies we'll focus on:

  • Regularly update your device's operating system and applications

    It's essential to keep your device's operating system and applications current. These updates often include critical security patches that fix vulnerabilities and defend your device against known threats.

  • Enable remote wipe capabilities

    Make sure remote wipe capabilities are activated on your device. This feature lets you erase sensitive data stored on your device remotely in case it's lost or stolen.
