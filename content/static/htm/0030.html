Tips to put safety measures in place

LESSON 3 of 9

We'll cover practical tips to enhance the security of your personal devices when using them for work. By following these recommendations, you can significantly reduce the risk of unauthorized access and data breaches. Here are four tips to keep in mind:

Tip 1

Make sure you create strong, unique passwords 

Using strong, unique passwords for each login helps to enhance the security of your accounts. Using the same password for multiple accounts and one gets compromised puts all your other accounts at risk.

Tip 2

Turn on encryption 

Encryption scrambles your data so that even if someone gets their hands on your device, they won't be able to access your personal information without the encryption key.

Tip 3

Install a reliable antivirus program

Install a trustworthy antivirus program on your device, and keep it updated. This way, you'll be able to detect and remove any harmful software. Regular updates ensure your antivirus software has the latest definitions to identify new threats, providing ongoing protection against malware and other malicious programs.

Tip 4

Stay away from unsecured Wi-Fi networks

Avoid connecting to unsecured Wi-Fi networks, especially when handling work-related tasks. Unsecured networks lack encryption, making it easier for hackers to intercept and access your data.
